# Backing Transparency

Osero Earn includes a **Transparency SDK** — a suite of live data feeds that gives stablecoin platforms and their users a real-time, auditable view of the assets, liquidity, and risk capital backing the Sky Savings Rate.

Stablecoin platforms can surface this data directly within their own product UI, enabling them to communicate the quality and safety of the underlying yield to their users without building any data infrastructure themselves.

The Transparency API is structured across four feeds:

## Position Monitoring

The Position Monitoring feed provides a detailed live view of the entire balance sheet backing the Sky Savings Rate.

Each position in the feed includes:

* **Net Asset Value (NAV)** — the real-time value of the position
* **Annualised yield** — how much yield is generated by this position
* **Risk capital requirements** — the amount of junior risk capital associated with the position
* **Yield mechanism explanation** — a plain-language description of how returns are generated by this position
* **Asset issuer links** — links to the underlying asset issuer and relevant documentation

This feed gives stablecoin platforms and their users full visibility into where the yield is coming from at any given moment, across all strategies managed by Agents operating in the Sky Ecosystem.

## Liquidity

The Liquidity feed provides a detailed view into the **liquid assets** backing the Sky Savings Rate, helping stablecoin platforms and users understand how readily the underlying collateral can be converted to meet redemption demand.

The feed distinguishes between two liquidity tiers:

### Idle stablecoins

Stablecoins held in reserve and immediately redeemable with no market impact. These represent the highest-quality liquidity available within the system.

### Onchain DeFi positions

Positions deployed in onchain DeFi protocols that can be readily liquidated with no price impact. These positions are liquid in practice, though redemption requires an onchain transaction.

For a full breakdown of how actively stabilising collateral functions within Sky Protocol's peg management framework, see [Liquidity & Actively Stabilising Collateral](position-liquidity.md).

## Risk Capital

The Risk Capital feed provides a live breakdown of all junior and senior risk capital positions under the Sky Savings Rate, broken down by entity.

This enables stablecoin platforms to communicate to their users the **loss-absorption structure** that protects depositors in the event of a position impairment — a meaningful trust signal for platforms targeting retail or institutional customers who are accustomed to the protections of regulated financial products.

For a full explanation of how the risk capital framework operates, see [Risk Capital](capital-protection.md).
